The "**Migrating Knowledgebase With AI**" OBS sub-series (Parts 3–7 = edit-19/20/21/22/23) has landed on grade **v7-contrast** (mid 0.46, deep shadows, white held, neutral warm colorbalance) EVERY time — its face-crop raw YAVG runs ~88–106 (bright; Part 7's front half up to ~114), so any mid-lift (lift66 → output ~110–125) blows the forehead and gets rejected. This is the OPPOSITE default from the "How to Avoid Losing Context" series, which is v7-lift 0.66 on its dimmer sources — see [[feedback-grade-brightness]].

**Why:** two concurrent OBS series with the same subject/backdrop but different exposure bands; auto-shipping one series' grade onto the other (or an earlier part's lift value) burns or darkens the face.

**How to apply:** for a "Migrating Knowledgebase" part, still measure the face crop + show the 5-up sheet (per [[feedback-grade-brightness]] rule), but expect v7-contrast to win. Measure EVERY kept segment, not just the brightest — Part 7's exposure drifted mid-take (see [[feedback-exposure-match-segments]]).
